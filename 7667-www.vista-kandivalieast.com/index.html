About Mahindra Vista

Mahindra Vista, a new project by Mahindra Lifespace near WEH Kandivali 101, presents thoughtfully designed 1, 2, 3, and 4 BHK apartments within a 10-acre township. Phase 2 is now launching, offering an array of unique amenities such as an urban forest, a pet park, walking and cycling paths, and a kids' play area. A seasonal pond with a pavilion, barbecue zones, outdoor gym, sports court, yoga deck, colonnade pathway, pools, and a skating park foster a vibrant, active community for all ages. Embrace an enhanced lifestyle surrounded by nature and modern conveniences.
