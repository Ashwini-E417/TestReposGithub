About Piramal Mahalaxmi

Piramal Mahalaxmi by Piramal Realty offers modern 2, 3, 4 & 4.5 BHK residences in South Mumbai with lifetime views of the Arabian Sea and Mahalaxmi Race Course. Spread over 4 acres with Spread over 4 acres, it features 3 iconic towers of 66 storeys. Exceptional amenities include a swimming pool, rooftop deck, clubhouse lounge, squash court, and more. Key nearby landmarks include Mahalaxmi Railway Station, Lower Parel, High Street Phoenix Mall, Wockhardt Hospital, Nariman Point (7.9 KM), and The St. Regis (2.0 KM).
